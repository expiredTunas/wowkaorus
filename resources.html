Website-making

Web stuff

These are resources that I've collected that will probably help with website-making or anything in relation to that (like carrds, rentries, etc.), I don't use some of them but it might be useful for some other people out there.

sadgrl.online

While this website will no longer be updated and has since been archived, it still has many wonderful resources when it comes to making your own website, like layout generators and all that stuff.

Blinkies.cafe

For generating awesome blinkies! I think that's how people get a lot of blinkies anyways!!! Or you can just take some from tumblr cuz a lot of them are cool for me!

Glitter Graphics

A resource full of community-made glitter graphics! Glitter graphics, or any kind of old webby graphics. Once again I also use tumblr for this, scour through neocities websites or go to gifcities which is a huge archive of graphics ripped STRAIGHT from the old webbies themselves!!!

Cursors-4u

Also pretty self-explanatory. Cursors for YOU!!! For people who also want to get custom cursors. You can also find cursors through deviantart too, or somewhere. I don't really know much about finding them.
